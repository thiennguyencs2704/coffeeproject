MELBOURNE

Melbourne serves as a gateway to many stunning natural attractions in the surrounding region. Day trips to the
Great Ocean Road, Phillip Island to see penguins, the Dandenong Ranges, and the Yarra Valley wine region are
popular options for visitors looking to explore the beautiful landscapes beyond the city.
