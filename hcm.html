HO CHI MINH CITY

Ho Chi Minh City has a wealth of historical sites that reflect its past. The War Remnants Museum is a must-visit, offering
exhibits related to the Vietnam War, providing insight into the country's history. The Independence Palace, also known
as Reunification Palace, is a historic building that played a significant role during the Vietnam War and is now a museum
open to visitors. The Saigon Central Post Office, built by the French in the 19th century, is an architectural gem that still
functions as a post office and is worth a visit.
